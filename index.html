Bio

Hello! I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at National University of Singapore, currently specializing in knowledge editing and AI interpretability for large language models. Previously, I have worked on UAV networks, IoT systems and computer vision.

I graduated from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India with B.E. in Electrical and Electronics. Previously, I've had the privilege of working at Microsoft and Flipkart. During my time at BITS Pilani, I interned at Sun Mobility and Draup.
